About Us

Welcome to Meriendahan sa Amianan, where we bring you the best of Ilocos' local snacks and delicacies. From our famous Special Empanada to hearty bowls of Mami, we aim to deliver an unforgettable culinary experience that keeps you coming back for more.

Our journey began with a passion for food and a desire to share the rich flavors of the north with everyone. Every dish is prepared with love and the finest ingredients to ensure quality and authenticity.

Whether you're dining in or taking out, we're here to serve you the taste of Amianan with pride and joy. Thank you for making us part of your snack time tradition!
